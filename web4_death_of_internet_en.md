# Web4.0 = Death of Internet

Working note.

---

This is a rough exploration of what happens when the web is no longer primarily for humans, but for AI.

The emergence of generative AI has fundamentally changed the nature of the web.

The web is no longer a place for human expression alone.

It is becoming a system where:

- AI reads
- AI writes
- AI consumes
- AI mediates

In this sense, Web4.0 may be understood as the “Death of Internet” — not in the sense of disappearance, but transformation.

---

## Key ideas

- Journalism may no longer be about writing articles
- The role of the journalist may dissolve
- “Noise” becomes more valuable than curated information
- Human expression becomes ubiquitous
- Platforms may be vertically integrated with AI systems
- Advertising transforms into recommendation
